How It Works

From Consultation to Savings in Four Steps

We handle everything from design to permits — your only job is to enjoy the savings.

01

Free Roof Analysis

Submit your address and electric bill details. Our engineers analyze your roof using satellite imaging to determine solar viability, sun exposure, and optimal panel placement — all at no cost to you.

02

Custom System Design

We design a system tailored to your home’s architecture and your family’s energy consumption patterns. You receive a detailed proposal with production estimates, savings projections, and fixed pricing.

03

Professional Installation

Our in-house, licensed Texas electricians handle the entire installation. Most residential systems are fully operational within 1–3 days. We manage all permits, inspections, and utility interconnection paperwork.

04

Start Saving Immediately

Once your system is activated, you’ll see savings from day one. Monitor your production in real-time through our mobile app and watch your electric meter spin backwards.

Average timeline: 3-4 weeks from consultation to activation

Our streamlined process means you can start saving within a month of your initial inquiry. Permitting timelines vary by municipality.
